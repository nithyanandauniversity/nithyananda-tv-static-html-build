Title: The Truth About Brahmacharya Part 1 Name Of The Convention: Shiva Sutras Session on: The dilemma of marriage versus Sannyas Date: 28 April 2006 Venue: Seattle, Washington, USA In this video Supreme Pontiff of Hinduism HDH #Nithyananda Paramashivam says anyone who enters into a spiritual life will always questions whether they should marry or become a Sannyasi. No Master is able to give a straight solution to this age old problem. For 3 years HDH studied and analyzed this question to determine what the best solution is for the modern man. The answer He delivers in this video series is not meant for those who feel spirituality is an option; the answer is for those who feel spirituality is compulsory in their life. Brahmacharya is more than just celibacy, the full meaning of the word is living like a God; which means living with reality and without fantasies and imagination. The solution to this age old question given by Shiva is for transformation without suppression.
