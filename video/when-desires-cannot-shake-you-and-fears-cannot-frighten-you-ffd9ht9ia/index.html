From the works of Living Enlightened Master Paramahamsa Nithyananda. This clip is taken from Shiva Sutras discourses titled, "Transcend through the body" and "Don't Think Be Aware". Swamiji in this clip tells us that once the click or initiation happens and when you come back down to the so-called human experience he now gives us an initiation to disconnect ourselves from our body or any other body to due to fear or greed. He gives us this initiation to allow the experience of living enlightenment to settle in us when we understand and internalize this Ananda Sutra.
